Problem

Students were context-switching between Discord, Notion, and classroom tools. The founders needed a single, opinionated space with low-latency AI support and clear navigation for peer sessions.

  • Latency targets set by device tier (mid Android first).
  • Navigation simplified to three core journeys: study session, flashcards, Q&A.
  • Guardrails to keep GPT outputs concise and cite sources.

Solution blueprint

  • Composable Nuxt UI kit with shells, empty states, and loading skeletons.
  • Supabase auth + storage with optimistic updates and realtime presence.
  • Gemini prompt scaffolds for contextual chat, flashcards, and recap.
  • Analytics cards surfacing streaks, time-on-task, and completion rate.
Role: product, UX, engineering Artifacts: flows, kit, QA matrix Collab: async + weekly sync

Experience highlights

Session HQ

Shared canvas with chat, pinned cards, and quick links to flashcards. Presence indicators keep partners in sync.

Flashcards + recap

AI-assisted flashcards with manual overrides, spaced repetition, and one-tap recap generation from notes.

Safety & trust

Moderation hooks, rate limits, and audit logging. Help center built-in with support macros.

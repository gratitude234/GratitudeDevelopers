JabuSpark — AI study hub

Student-focused workspace with contextual chat, flashcards, and collaborative notes.

Overview

Gratitude Developers partnered with the JabuSpark team to build an AI-assisted study hub in under two weeks, balancing realtime collaboration with fast page loads on mid-tier devices.

Problem

Students needed a single place to study together without slow load times or confusing navigation. The founders wanted a demo-ready product for an accelerator and needed clear IA plus reliable authentication.

Process

  • Mapped key journeys (study sessions, flashcards, Q&A) and simplified IA.
  • Built a Nuxt UI kit with reusable panels, states, and empty screens.
  • Implemented Supabase auth/storage with optimistic updates and logging.
  • Ran two feedback loops with Loom walkthroughs before handoff.

Outcome

Launched within 12 days, kept LCP under 900ms on mid-tier Android, and improved study task completion time by 18% after the UI refactor.

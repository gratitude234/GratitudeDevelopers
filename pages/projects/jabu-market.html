Case Study

Jabu Market — Campus vendor marketplace

Vue marketplace with PHP APIs, secure checkouts, inventory, and vendor analytics.

Back to all projects Book a call about a similar build

Overview

Gratitude Developers delivered a campus-ready marketplace with reliable checkout, vendor dashboards, and admin controls, keeping performance smooth on modest data plans.

Problem

Campus vendors were struggling with manual inventory and slow onboarding. The team needed a trusted checkout flow, clear analytics, and faster time-to-first-sale.

Process

  • Documented roles and permissions for vendors, admins, and students.
  • Built Vue front-end against PHP APIs with clear contract testing.
  • Added guided onboarding, inventory imports, and order notifications.
  • Instrumented dashboards with the metrics vendors care about most.

Outcome

Reduced vendor onboarding time from 3 days to same-day with guided flows and made checkout latency predictable even during campus spikes.

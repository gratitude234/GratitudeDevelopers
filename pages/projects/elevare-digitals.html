Elevare Digitals — Portfolio refresh

Portfolio with case studies, testimonials, and lead capture focused on conversions.

Back to all projects Book a call about a similar build

Overview

Gratitude Developers rebuilt the Elevare portfolio to showcase wins, testimonials, and offers with a clear conversion-focused narrative.

Problem

The previous site buried proof points and had unclear CTAs, leading to drop-offs from paid traffic and low form completion.

Process

  • Reorganized navigation and case study templates for clarity.
  • Added social proof blocks, lead magnets, and FAQ modules.
  • Optimized media handling for faster renders on mobile.
  • Set up analytics events to track conversions across the funnel.

Outcome

30% lift in lead form completion after the UX refresh, with faster page loads and clearer calls to action.

Case Study

Seabird Law Firm — Legal services site

Nuxt marketing site with SEO copy, attorney bios, and contact automations.

Back to all projects Book a call about a similar build

Overview

Gratitude Developers rebuilt Seabird’s site to spotlight practice areas, improve lead quality, and keep content flexible for the internal team.

Problem

The prior site buried CTAs and lacked schema markup, resulting in low conversions from organic visitors and inconsistent intake workflows.

Process

  • Interviewed partners to map the ideal consultation journey.
  • Drafted SEO-friendly copy and structured data for practice pages.
  • Built reusable components for bios, FAQs, and testimonials.
  • Automated intake notifications and calendar routing to the right attorney.

Outcome

Increased booked consultations by 22% with clearer CTAs, search-friendly content, and smoother intake.
